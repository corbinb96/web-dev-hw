What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
  An IP address is the address assigned to any computer. DNS is Domain Name Service, it converts alphabetic references into the IP address of a server.
What is a cookie?
  a cookie is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored on the user's computer by the user's web browser
List the the three basic parts of a URL.
  The protocol, the domain name, and the path
